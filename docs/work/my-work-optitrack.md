# OptiTrack

OptiTrack is a motion capture hardware and software company. When I first started at OptiTrack I converted our outdated Wiki doc site to the more modern GitBook platform and over time curated and updated the documentation to more closely reflect the brand and the audience. This demonstrates my ability to independently lead a content management project to elevate an organizations user-facing documentation.

## OptiTrack Audience

Since OptiTrack spans several industries (Entertainment, Robotics, Biomechanics, etc.), I made an effort to ensure anyone new to motion capture and our product had the opportunity to understand and build upon complex concepts. I am a firm believer in visuals from photos to diagrams in order to accomplish bridging the gap between experienced and beginner audiences.
